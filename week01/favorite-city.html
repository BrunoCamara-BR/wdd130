The favorite City

My city is called Sete Lagoas, it is a medium-sized city in the State of Minas Gerais, Brazil. The city is 72 kilometers from the capital city, Belo Horizonte. The city has more than 237,931 inhabitants.

Sete Lagoas is a peaceful city, there are a lot of schools, markets, hospitals, and industries, which generate a lot of jobs for the inhabitants.
